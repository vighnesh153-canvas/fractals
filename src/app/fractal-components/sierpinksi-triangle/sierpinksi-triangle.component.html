Sierpinski's triangle is quite a popular fractal. It can be implemented by a simple recursion algorithm. But, here I share with you a Chaos Game approach.

The approach is pretty straight forward. Choose 3 points: which will act as the vertices of the triangle. Then, choose a random point, literally anywhere on the plane. Choose any one point at random out of the 3 vertices of the triangle. Now, plot the point which lies exactly in between the random vertex and the random point. Update our random point to coordinates of the plotted point. Now, repeat this process. This will eventually create a figure that looks like a Sierpinski's triangle. How wonderful!
